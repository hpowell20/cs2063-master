Creating sample data:

import nltk

words = sorted([[x, "; ".join([y.definition for y in nltk.corpus.wordnet.synsets(x)])] for x in nltk.corpus.wordnet.all_lemma_names() if x.isalpha() and x.startswith('ab')])

import json
outf = open('data.json', 'w')
json.dump(words, outf)
outf.close()

For fun, consider changing to all words that contain the word 'duck':

words = sorted([[x.replace('_', ' '), "; ".join([y.definition for y in nltk.corpus.wordnet.synsets(x)])] for x in nltk.corpus.wordnet.all_lemma_names() if 'duck' in x])




Then it can be a "ducktionary"!!
